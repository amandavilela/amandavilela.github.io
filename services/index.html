How I can help

I help companies and teams improve frontend performance, accessibility, and architecture so their products scale efficiently and deliver better user experiences.

This includes optimizing Core Web Vitals, addressing usability and compliance issues, and bringing clarity to technical decisions as complexity grows. I also support teams in improving consistency, speed, and overall code quality.

Core areas

Performance & Core Web Vitals

Improve loading speed, stability, and responsiveness with direct impact on user experience and business metrics.

  • Diagnose LCP, CLS, and INP
  • Identify bottlenecks (JavaScript, rendering, third parties)
  • Provide a prioritized optimization plan

Accessibility (WCAG)

Make your product more usable, inclusive, and aligned with accessibility standards.

  • Accessibility audits
  • Identify critical issues and risks
  • Provide clear guidance for improvements

Frontend architecture & teams

Improve how your frontend is structured and how your team makes technical decisions.

  • Architecture and codebase review
  • Definition of standards and best practices
  • Support for design systems and rendering strategies
  • Guidance for team structure and technical decision-making

Engagement formats

The way we work together depends on your goals, context, and the level of support required. These are the most common engagement models:

  • Audit

    A focused evaluation of your product or codebase with clear findings and next steps.

    Best when you need to understand what's wrong and what to prioritize.

    • Performance (Core Web Vitals) or accessibility audits
    • Identification of key issues and risks
    • Prioritized recommendations for improvement

  • Project-based work

    Hands-on support for specific initiatives with defined scope and timeline.

    Best when you need senior expertise to move something forward or solve a critical problem.

    • Performance optimization
    • Accessibility improvements
    • Architecture changes or refactoring
    • Collaboration with your team to unblock delivery

  • Retainer (ongoing support)

    Continuous support integrated into your team's workflow.

    Best when your team needs consistent technical guidance without hiring full-time.

    • Async support (e.g. Slack)
    • Technical direction and decision support
    • Ongoing reviews and recommendations

  • Workshops & talks

    Structured sessions for teams or events.

    Best when you want to upskill your team or share knowledge at scale.

    • Performance & Core Web Vitals
    • Accessibility
    • Frontend architecture and best practices

  • 1:1 or group sessions

    Flexible sessions focused on your specific context.

    Best for quick feedback, problem-solving, or targeted guidance.

    • Technical questions
    • Project discussions
    • Career or growth direction
